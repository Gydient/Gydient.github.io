How to be a good dog

I'm a French DOg of Gydient. Now she is continuing her bachelor at Hamburg Design Department. The French Bulldog (French: bouledogue or bouledogue français) is a breed of domestic dog, bred to be companion dogs.

The breed is the result of a cross between Toy Bulldogs imported from England and local ratters in Paris, France, in the 1800s. They are stocky, compact dogs with a friendly, mild-mannered temperament.
